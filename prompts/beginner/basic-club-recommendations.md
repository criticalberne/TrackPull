# Basic Club Recommendations

**Skill Level:** Beginner
**Goal:** Find out what category of clubs (game improvement, players distance, players, etc.) best suits your swing based on your data.

## How to Use

1. Export your CSV from TrackPull
2. Open ChatGPT or Claude and either:
   - **Drag and drop** your CSV file into the chat window (easiest), or
   - **Paste** your CSV data directly into the prompt where indicated
3. Copy the prompt below and send it along with your data

---

```
I'm looking for help choosing the right type of golf clubs based on my launch monitor data. I'm not sure if I should be using game improvement irons, players distance irons, or players irons — or what driver/fairway wood characteristics suit me best.

Based on my data, please:

1. Categorize my skill level based on the numbers (club speed, consistency, dispersion)
2. Recommend what category of irons would suit me (game improvement, players distance, players)
3. Suggest what driver loft range would work for my swing speed and launch conditions
4. Mention whether I'd benefit from hybrids replacing long irons
5. Keep the explanation simple — I'm not a club fitting expert

My shot data is included below (or attached as a CSV file).
```

---

## Tips for Reading the Response

- **Game improvement clubs** have larger heads, more forgiveness, and help get the ball in the air — great if your speeds are moderate or your strike is inconsistent
- **Players distance clubs** blend some forgiveness with a more compact look — a good middle ground
- **Players clubs** offer maximum control but demand consistent center-face contact
- If your long iron carry distances overlap with your mid irons, hybrids are probably a good swap
- This is a starting point — always hit clubs in person before buying if you can
