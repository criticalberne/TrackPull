### Non-Blocking Integration Note

Phase 11 `include-averages-checkbox` change handler does not call Phase 12 `updatePreview()`. This is architecturally correct: `writeTsv()` (used by preview) produces shots-only output and has no `includeAverages` parameter. The export toggle controls `writeCsv()` only. If `writeTsv` ever gains averages support, the missing `updatePreview()` call site would cause a silent regression.
