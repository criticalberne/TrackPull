<objective>
Add an "Include averages and consistency in export" checkbox to the popup that controls whether CSV exports contain Average and Consistency summary rows. Wire the checkbox state to chrome.storage.local and read it in the service worker's existing EXPORT_CSV_REQUEST handler, replacing the hardcoded `true` in the `writeCsv` call.

Purpose: Users who want only raw shot data can exclude summary rows from exports without losing the option for users who want them.
Output: Checkbox in popup, storage persistence, service worker reads preference, unit tests for writeCsv toggle behavior.
</objective>
